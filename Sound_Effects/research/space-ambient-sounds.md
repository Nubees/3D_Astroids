**Floating in Space by Umplix** — designed specifically for game void scenes between gameplay segments.

### Technical Considerations
1. **Looping:** For gameplay ambience, choose tracks with seamless loops. Kenney's packs include loop markers for most ambient pieces.
2. **Volume mixing:** Ambient should sit at -18 to -24 dB below gameplay SFX so it doesn't compete with explosions/fires.
3. **Format:** Use OGG Vorbis for web deployment (smaller than WAV, quality is comparable). Keep WAV originals in source folder.
4. **Crossfade:** When transitioning between menu ambience and gameplay ambience, use a 2-3 second crossfade to avoid jarring silence gaps.
