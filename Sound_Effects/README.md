## Quick Recommendations

### Best Overall Pick (Zero Legal Risk, No Attribution)
**Kenney.nl's Space Shooter Remastered + Sci-fi Sounds packs**
- CC0 / Public Domain
- Professional quality, purpose-built for space games
- Complete audio suite: ambient, SFX, UI sounds

### For Authentic Retro Feel
Study the **555 timer circuit descriptions** in `research/original-asteroids-sfx.md` and create original analog-synthesis versions. The original Asteroids sounds were generated by discrete analog electronics — a faithful recreation would use real oscillator chips or an analog-modeled synth.

### Legal Note on Original Asteroids SFX
The 1979 Atari Asteroids sounds were **analog circuits**, not digital recordings. The circuit descriptions are public domain information (from schematics), but the specific audio recordings from arcade machines may be under copyright. For a commercial project, recreate the sounds using the circuit recipes rather than sampling original arcade SFX.
